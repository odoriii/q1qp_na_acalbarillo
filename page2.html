Study Background

Our group has chosen to analyze the topic of Happiness Ratings of different years from 2018 to 2022 and we categorized them into three phases: pandemic years, pre-pandemic, and post pandemic. Well, you might wonder why we chose this topics and it is because we want to understand the Covid-19 pandemic's impact on people's happiness and emotional well-being. As we go beyond our data, there are different years so we aim to gain a deep understanding how pandemic influence happiness rating given its disruptive effects on health and daily life.
